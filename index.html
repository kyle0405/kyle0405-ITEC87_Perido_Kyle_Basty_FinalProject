Welcome to my Final Project!

This project demonstrates the creation of a main page that features a clean and user-friendly interface with buttons directing users to four different activity pages. Each button on the main page represents a unique activity, designed to amazed your creativity.

Certificate of Registration

A Certificate of Registration for a school typically refers to a document that confirms the enrollment or registration of a student in a particular educational institution or program. It serves as an official record of the student's affiliation with the school for a specific academic period or course of study.

Resume/ Curriculum Vitae

A Resume or Curriculum Vitae (CV) is a document that summarizes a person's education, work experience, skills, achievements, and other qualifications for employment or academic opportunities. It serves as a marketing tool for individuals seeking job positions, internships, scholarships, or academic programs.


Restaurant Menu

A Restaurant Menu typically refers to a list of Filipino and other nationalities' food and beverage items available for purchase at a restaurant. It serves as a primary communication tool between the restaurant and its customers from different countries, detailing the variety of main dishes, vegetables, desserts, drinks, and add-ons offered by the establishment.


Sari-Sari Store

The term Sari-sari Store embodies the concept of diversity and inclusivity, reflecting the store's purpose of providing a variety of items to meet the diverse needs and preferences of customers. These items can include Jeans, T-shirts, Perfumes, Sandos, Toys, sometimes even cooked food or freshly prepared meals, and other things that are essential and beneficial to customers.
